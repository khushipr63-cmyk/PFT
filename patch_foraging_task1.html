Patch Foraging Task

Welcome to the Patch Foraging Task!

• In this task, you will see a tree patch with berries displayed on the screen.

• You can either choose to stay in the same patch and collect berries, or leave and travel to a new patch.

• Moving to a new patch will incur a travel time of either 5, 10, or 20 seconds.

• Your goal is to collect the maximum amount of berries in 8 minutes.

• Press SPACE to stay in the patch.

• Press L to leave the patch.

Press SPACE to begin.
